Radianite

The mysterious material

Some people asking what is radianite?, Let us explain. Radianite is an unknown material that come out from an event that change our life which name is First Light.

Radianite extracted from mine and then processed and put in the Radianite Box. Radianite is clean, safe source energy, and renewable energy.

Exposure to radiation for a long time was reported to result in "unusual feats of strength" in affected people. In other cases, it could inflict more permanent supernatural abilities upon certain people, who would become known as Radiants.

Radianite has been used to power and augment much of the Earths' technology since its discovery. Some devices require a Radi-Core, a cube that contains solid radianite, to be powered. A Radi-Core can be super-heated to liquefy the radianite inside, which will cause it to generate even more power. Radianite technology also offers a multitude of "space faring opportunities".

Radianite is capable of generating enormous amounts of energy, and through the Kingdom Radianite Power Program, we are responsible for powering more than 3/4 of the world.
